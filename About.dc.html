Why it exists

Farmers solve real problems faster when good experience is easy to find.

Much of the most useful agricultural knowledge is still trapped in phone calls, WhatsApp threads, field days, and private notes. That knowledge is valuable because it is specific: a crop, a soil type, a season, a province, a mistake that cost money, a small change that worked.

AkkerBooster turns those scattered conversations into a searchable, respectful, region-aware knowledge base built around farmers rather than products.
